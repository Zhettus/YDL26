HOW TO PLAY

1
Pick a missionRead the contract carefully. It says what the robot must do and what it needs — strength (torque), sensors, and a power limit. You have 90 seconds per round!
2
Drag parts onto the chassisThe left panel is your parts store. Each part costs credits. Drag a card into one of the 6 chassis slots to add it to your robot.
3
Watch the Build Analysis panelIt updates live. Green bars = requirement met. Red = something's missing or over the power limit. Fix it before deploying.
4
Deploy and earn pointsHit ⚡ DEPLOY ROBOT. Pass the test and you earn the contract reward. Leftover budget = efficiency bonus points!
GLOSSARY
🔩 TORQUERobot strength — ability to push, pull, or lift. Motors add torque. More motors = more torque.
⚡ POWER DRAWTotal watts all parts consume. Stay under the contract's limit or the circuit overloads.
👁 SENSORSLet the robot perceive its world. Line sensor = floor markings. Camera = objects. Grip = grabbing.
💰 CREDITSYour budget per round (200 cr). Leftover credits after a win earn bonus score points.
