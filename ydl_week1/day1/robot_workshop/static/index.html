HOW TO PLAY

1
Pick a mission
Read the contract carefully. It tells you what the robot must do and what it needs — strength (torque), sensors, and a power limit.
2
Drag parts onto the chassis
The left panel is your parts store. Each part costs credits. Drag a part card into one of the 6 chassis slots to add it to your robot.
3
Watch the Build Analysis panel
It updates live. Green bars = requirement met. Red = something's missing or over the limit. Fix it before deploying.
4
Deploy and earn credits
Hit ⚡ DEPLOY ROBOT. Pass the test and you earn the contract reward. Leftover budget = efficiency bonus!
GLOSSARY
🔩 TORQUE How strong your robot is — its ability to push, pull, or lift. Motors add torque. More motors = more torque.
⚡ POWER DRAW Total watts all your parts consume. Every part uses power. Stay under the contract's limit or the circuit overloads.
👁 SENSORS Sensors let the robot perceive its environment. Line sensor = sees floor markings. Camera = sees objects. Grip sensor = in the gripper arm.
💰 CREDITS Your shopping budget. You start each round with 200 cr. Leftover credits after a successful build earn you bonus score points.
